A Peaceful Retreat in Santiniketan

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Bolpur, Udita Homestay (Prayas) offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional hospitality. Whether you are visiting Santiniketan for leisure, festivals or family vacations, our homestay provides a peaceful environment that feels just like home.


We believe every guest deserves personalized care. From well-appointed air-conditioned rooms to delicious home-cooked meals, every detail has been thoughtfully arranged to make your stay memorable.

Explore Santiniketan

Discover the culture, history and natural beauty surrounding Udita Homestay.

Experience the Cultural Heart of Bengal

Santiniketan is renowned for its heritage, art, music and literature. During your stay, explore the famous Visva-Bharati campus, Sonajhuri Haat, Khoai, Amar Kutir and many other attractions located just a short distance from the homestay.


We also assist our guests with Toto and car bookings so that travelling around Santiniketan remains comfortable and hassle-free.
